Marketing · Tech · Logistics Jun – Jul 2026
Interactive FVL Compound Network Map
CEVA Logistics — Finished Vehicle Logistics Europe
Context

CEVA Logistics' FVL Europe sales teams relied on static PowerPoint slides to present their compound network to clients — a format that lacked interactivity, flexibility and visual impact. As part of my internship, I identified this gap and proposed building a modern, interactive sales tool.

Objective

Design and develop a digital tool allowing sales teams to explore and showcase CEVA's European FVL network in real time during client meetings, replacing static slides with a product-grade interactive experience.

What I Built
  • Interactive map of 39 FVL compounds across 15 European countries, built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Leaflet.js — fully aligned with CEVA's brand identity
  • Dynamic compound dashboards with services, connectivity, certifications, and capacity data — sourced from a structured CSV database
  • Country highlight mode with zoom animation, compound labels, and a sidebar listing all compounds per country
  • Smart filtering by Port, Rail, EV Charging, and Workshop availability
  • Companion Python script allowing any team member to update the map by editing the CSV — no coding required
  • Complete documentation package (README, tutorial slide) left for the team
Approach

Developed in collaboration with Claude (Anthropic's AI assistant), which I used as a technical partner throughout — directing the architecture, iterating on the UI, and driving all product decisions. End-to-end ownership from concept to delivery.

Impact — Delivered a tool that transforms how CEVA's FVL sales teams present their network — moving from a static slide deck to a dynamic, client-facing digital experience. Fully autonomous, maintainable by non-technical staff, and ready to scale.
